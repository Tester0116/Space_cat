FAQ

Frequency Asked Questions

What is Solana staking?

Participating in Solana staking involves engaging in the Proof of Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism of the Solana blockchain network. By staking Solana (SOL) tokens, users actively contribute to the network's security and, in turn, receive rewards.

What do staking rewards entail?

Staking rewards refer to incentives provided to individuals who stake their cryptocurrency to support a blockchain network. In the context of Solana, these rewards are usually expressed in SOL tokens and are distributed to stakers based on the size of their stake. These incentives act as a motivation for users to hold and stake their tokens, contributing to the decentralization and security of the network.

What is MEV?

Maximal Extractable Value (MEV) represents the added value that participants in the DeFi ecosystem, known as MEV searchers, can gain by influencing the inclusion and ordering of transactions in blocks produced by validators. Various activities like arbitrage, front-running, NFT sniping, sandwich trading, and collateralized position liquidation, which are prevalent in any DeFi ecosystem, contribute to MEV. Searchers are willing to pay extra fees to gain priority access to MEV opportunities. These fees, referred to as "MEV rewards," have the potential to generate substantial additional revenue for validators and their delegators. Our validator, powered by Jito software, supports and utilizes this technology.

What is the frequency of distributing staking rewards?

Staking rewards are distributed at the conclusion of each epoch, occurring approximately once every ~2 days. These rewards are automatically credited to the balance of the stake account.

How can I stake Solana?

Alternatively, you can use staking.kiwi, click "Connect wallet," and follow the provided instructions. Staking SOL tokens is possible through various wallets, platforms, or directly using the Solana Command Line Interface (CLI).

Certainly, in most wallets and platforms, you have the flexibility to unstake your SOL tokens at any time.
